Splash White Water Rafting Co.

History

Founded in 1995 with a single raft and an audacious dream, Splash White Water Rafting Co. began as a small local operation driven by pure passion for the river. Our founders, lifelong adventurers, saw the Nile's potential to deliver world-class white water experiences and set out to share that magic with the world.
