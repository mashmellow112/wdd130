Barcelona, Spain

Barcelona is my favourite city because of its vibrant street life, stunning architecture by Antoni Gaudí and the relaxed pace of life on the Mediterranean coast. There is always something to see or do, from wandering the Gothic Quarter to relaxing on Barceloneta Beach.

The food is amazing – fresh seafood, tapas and the famous paella are just the beginning. I love the mix of old and new, the art museums, the mountain views from Park Güell and the football atmosphere at Camp Nou.
