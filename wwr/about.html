White Water Rafting

Experience the thrill of a lifetime with Splash White Water Rafting Co.! Nestled along the legendary banks of the Nile River in Jinja, Uganda, we offer world-class white water adventures.

History

Founded in 1995 with a single raft and an audacious dream, Splash White Water Rafting Co. began as a small local operation driven by pure passion for the river. Our founders saw the Nile's potential and set out to share that magic with the world.

Today, we proudly navigate over 500 miles of world-class whitewater every season, having grown into one of East Africa's most trusted rafting companies. We've transformed countless first-time rafters into passionate river enthusiasts.
