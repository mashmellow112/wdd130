White Water Rafting

At Splash White Water Rafting, every trip is an unforgettable river adventure. Our team of expert guides leads guests through thrilling rapids, lush riverside scenery, and the unique wildlife of Uganda’s Nile corridor. Whether you are a first-time rafter or a seasoned paddler, we tailor each excursion for excitement, safety, and unforgettable memories.

History

Founded in 2010 along the Nile River in Jinja, Splash White Water Rafting began with a small group of local guides and a shared love for the river. Over the years, we have grown into a trusted adventure company known for superior safety, authentic experiences, and strong community partnerships.

Today, our company combines modern rafting equipment with deep local knowledge. We offer guided river trips, custom packages, and family-friendly tours designed to showcase the natural beauty and cultural richness of Uganda while keeping guests comfortable and secure on every run.
