White Water Rafting

Experience the thrill of a lifetime with Splash White Water Rafting Co.! Nestled along the legendary banks of the Nile River in Jinja, Uganda, we offer world-class white water adventures that will awaken your spirit and push your limits. Whether you're a first-time rafter or a seasoned pro, our expert guides ensure safety while delivering heart-pounding excitement through Class IV and V rapids. Join us for an unforgettable journey on the wild waters!

Our History

Founded in 1995 with a single raft and an audacious dream, Splash White Water Rafting Co. began as a small local operation driven by pure passion for the river. Our founders, lifelong adventurers, saw the Nile's potential to deliver world-class white water experiences and set out to share that magic with the world.

Today, we proudly navigate over 500 miles of world-class whitewater every season, having grown from that humble beginning into one of East Africa's most trusted rafting companies. We've transformed countless first-time rafters into passionate river enthusiasts, building a community of adventure seekers who return year after year.
